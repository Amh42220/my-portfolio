About

I am a Junior Full-Stack Developer with a strong passion for software development and modern web technologies. My journey in technology began through self-learning and hands-on projects, where I developed a solid foundation in frontend development, backend APIs, and database systems .

I was honored to receive a scholarship sponsored by the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications and the CBRD FUND, which allowed me to join the ANT Technology Training Center. During this intensive 13-month training program, I gained practical experience in full-stack web development, software engineering principles, teamwork, and real-world project development. The program strengthened both my technical knowledge and problem-solving skills through continuous hands-on practice.

Today, I continue to improve my skills in JavaScript, Node.js, Vue.js, MySQL, and modern development tools while building personal and academic projects. My goal is to become a skilled software engineer who creates reliable, scalable, and user-friendly applications that solve real-world problems. I am committed to continuous learning, embracing new technologies, and growing into a professional developer who can contribute to innovative technology solutions.
